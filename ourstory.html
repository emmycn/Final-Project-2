Our Story

Who We Are

Anastasiia, the founder and creative mind behind byAnastasiia, developed a love for design and sewing in her early years. This passion ignited when she first engaged with her grandmother's sewing machine, pressing down on the pedals. With a clear vision for her life, she departed from school early to explore the global wonders of the sewing industry. This brand draws inspiration from her meaningful connections with her grandmother. As the saying goes, the rest is history.

Our Approach

We believe in authenticity and individuality. Our commitment to creating experiences that go beyond the ordinary ensures your garment is not just clothing but a narrative woven with care and creativity.
